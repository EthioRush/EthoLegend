Welcome to Abune Gorgorios School

Mission Statement

Our mission is to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to become responsible global citizens and lifelong learners. We aim to cultivate critical thinking, creativity, and leadership qualities within each student, preparing them to navigate the challenges and opportunities of the modern world.

At Abune Gorgorios School, we maintain high academic standards and offer a comprehensive curriculum designed to cater to the diverse learning needs of our students.

Holistic Development

Education extends beyond the classroom walls. That's why we prioritize holistic development by offering a wide range of extracurricular activities, including sports, arts, music, and community service initiatives.

Dedicated Faculty

Our team of experienced and dedicated educators is committed to providing personalized attention and support to every student.

Community Engagement

At the heart of Abune Gorgorios School is a vibrant and inclusive community where students, parents, teachers, and staff collaborate and support each other.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

Our campus boasts state-of-the-art facilities designed to enhance the learning experience and promote student well-being.

Admissions

We welcome students from diverse backgrounds and cultures, fostering a rich and inclusive learning environment where everyone feels valued and respected.
